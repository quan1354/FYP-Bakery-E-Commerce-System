Our Brand

From the initials of the bakers, L and E, combining with pan, which means bread in Japanese, we want to honour the origins of our baking journey through the brand name. Born in Penang in 2020 for the love of our quaint little island, we want to create our legacy here by offering high-quality, delicious baked goods through organic and all-natural ingredients to bring gastronomy enjoyment to our customers. We hope you find something that you like!

Our Bread

Our bread and loaves are from self-cultivated yeast that requires much care and love. Naturally cultivated yeast breaks down harmful enzymes like lectin, gluten and phytates in grains. With long fermentation hours (we use at least 72 hours), the natural bacteria and wild yeast digest the complex starches and sugar. The longer the dough is fermented, the more it breaks down.
Along with Japanese baking method, it produces more digestable, soft and fluffy bread, which is also preferred for Asian consumption. Enjoy LEPAN baked goods without worries as they are vegan―free from eggs and dairy.

Bread Storage

  • ROOM TEMPERATURE
    Keep in an airtight container for up to 3 days
  • FREEZER
    Store bread in an airtight bag or container. For loaves, slice the bread first before storing in freezer. Consume within 2 weeks for best freshness.

Reheat Bread

  • PAN
    Warm the bread in a pan on low heat for 3-5 minutes on each side.
  • OVEN
    Preheat oven at 180ºC. Bake the bread for 3-5 minutes.

organic ingredients and natural colour

We believe the most authentic bread tastes comes from the natural food ingredients prepared right. All the fresh produce we use are organic and biodynamic, with some from local small-scale farmers. This is our way of supporting local organic farmers for a more sustainable supply chain.

Our bread and ingredients used are free from additives, GMO, artificial flavouring and colouring, preservatives and food enhancers. The eye-pleasing colours and fantastic flavours in our bread are from fruits and vegetables. You can also taste the aroma of the grains in our organic wheat flour

premium whole grain flours

We use organic flours Made from wholesome grains grown organically at the foothill of the German Alps, Aichermuhle, one of the oldest family-owned companies in Bavaria since 1461. Choosing whole-grain flours with no preservatives or additives over refined flours ensures that the nutrient-rich contents are preserved in our baked goods.

high oleic organic cooking oil

We use Emile Noel organic cooking oil from France in our baking. Made from 100% high oleic sunflower seed oil, the cold-pressed cooking oil is high in omega-9, which is essential fatty acids considered to help in reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease and stroke, but low in omega-3 and 6. Emile Noel cooking oil is certified organic and has 100 years of history. This cold pressed oil retains its original flavour and nutrients through traditional screw press technique. It has a high smoke point, making it a healthy option for most types of cooking, including baking.
